This exercise is about typing correctly. Below you will find texts of different lengths with special characters. Try to type the texts correctly into the corresponding input field. If you want to check your input, press the "Check" button:

  • If there is an error, the correct part of the text appears below the input box right until the incorrect part.
  • If everything is correct, a green frame will appear and you can continue with the next block.

If all blocks are typed correctly, you can finish the test at the bottom of the page and check your time.
Do the test more often and try to become faster.
